# LoopUp Global Voice

**Type**: blog
**Word Count**: 1000

## Audience

Enterprise IT, UC, and digital transformation decision-makers operating across multiple geographies.

## Goal

Explain the business and operational friction caused by historically fragmented global telephony, then show how LoopUp reduces that friction by acting as a single global telco provider for Microsoft Teams Phone.

Primary angle: Operator Connect for Microsoft Teams Phone.

## Brief

Article that highlights the traditional fragmentation problem with global telephony historically needing local PBXs and local carriers, a struggle for multi-national companies. And how LoopUp approach the problem to offer a mature and capable global cloud telephony solution for teams. Picking out the tangible business and operational benefits of LoopUp being the single telco provider, instead of a patch work of different suppliers, contracts, terms, prices etc.
